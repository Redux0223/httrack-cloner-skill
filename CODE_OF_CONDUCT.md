# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Be respectful, specific, and evidence-driven. Critique code and behavior, not people. Share reproducible technical context and respect the ownership of website content used in examples.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, deliberate exposure of private information, and submission of stolen or unauthorized material are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Repository maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and community interactions that violate this standard. Serious or repeated violations may result in a temporary or permanent ban from project spaces.
